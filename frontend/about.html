Welcome to DinaStyleKnits, where handmade knitted accessories meet coastal inspiration. With a childhood spent near the sea in an industrial Ukrainian city, my creative spirit was ignited from an early age. Guided by my craftswoman mother, I learned the art of knitting and sewing, which remained cherished hobbies throughout my life.

When my daughter was born, I found more time to dedicate to knitting, and it soon became a profession that allowed me to combine my love for handmade creations with my family responsibilities. In 2011, I took a leap and sold my first knitted hats and scarves under the name Dina-style. The positive response and growing passion led me to open my Etsy shop in 2014, where I have had the pleasure of connecting with the vibrant creative community.

At DinaStyleKnits, each hand-knitted accessory is made with utmost care and attention to detail. I take pride in using only quality yarn and creating my own designs, ensuring that every piece is unique and made in limited numbers. Customer satisfaction is my priority, and I strive to provide exceptional service to each valued client. Beyond knitting and sewing, I embrace a holistic lifestyle and find inspiration in practices like yoga and Chinese qigong, infusing a sense of balance and harmony into my creations.

Thank you for visiting DinaStyleKnits, where you can discover beautiful and exclusive handmade accessories that reflect my coastal roots and passion for craftsmanship.
